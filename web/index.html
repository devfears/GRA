Costco-Style Tuxedo Chocolate Mousse Cake

Recreate the beloved Costco Tuxedo Chocolate Mousse Cake at home with this detailed recipe. This dessert features layers of rich chocolate cake sandwiching dark chocolate mousse and white chocolate mousse, all enrobed in a glossy chocolate ganache. Perfect for sitting down and eating off in a single day.

Cake Layer Ingredients

  • 1 box chocolate fudge cake mix
  • 1 extra egg (in addition to box requirements)
  • Melted butter (to replace oil in box instructions)
  • Milk (to replace water in box instructions)

Dark Chocolate Mousse Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips (4 oz)
  • 1/3 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup dark cocoa powder
  • 1 1/3 cups cold heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar

White Chocolate Mousse Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white chocolate chips
  • 2 cups heavy cream (divided)

Ganache Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup plus 1 tablespoon dark chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons room temperature butter
  • 1 tablespoon corn syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cake Layers:
    • Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)
    • Prepare cake mix according to box, but add one extra egg, use melted butter instead of oil, and milk instead of water
    • Bake in two 9-inch pans for about 20 minutes
    • Cool completely and level cake tops (save crumbs for decoration)
  2. Make Dark Chocolate Mousse:
    • Melt dark chocolate chips
    • Mix warm water with cocoa powder and add to melted chocolate
    • Whip cold heavy cream with powdered sugar until stiff peaks form
    • Fold chocolate mixture into whipped cream
  3. Prepare White Chocolate Mousse:
    • Melt white chocolate chips with 1 cup heavy cream
    • Whip remaining cold heavy cream until stiff peaks form
    • Fold melted white chocolate mixture into whipped cream
  4. Assembly:
    • Place first cake layer on serving plate
    • Spread dark chocolate mousse over cake
    • Add optional chopped brownie pieces
    • Chill for 1 hour
    • Spread white chocolate mousse layer
    • Top with second cake layer
    • Chill overnight or at least 8 hours
  5. Make and Apply Ganache:
    • Heat heavy cream and pour over chocolate chips
    • Add butter and corn syrup
    • Stir until smooth
    • Pour over chilled cake
    • Press reserved cake crumbs into sides
